Squad up India INFORMATION REGISTER

Final spot for the PUBG MOBILE SQUAD UP INDIA is now open. Register now for SQUI GCC CUP!

REGISTRATION PERIOD:
11/01/2019-15/01/2019

TOURNAMENT STRUCTURE:

Registered teams will be divided into groups and will compete against each another between 22/01/2019 & 24/01/2019.
Advancing teams will travel to MEXICO for SQUI GCC CUP Finals on 01/02/2019

REWARDS:

  1. Cash Prizes: 1st Place - USD 5,000 / 2nd Place - USD 3,000 / 3rd Place - USD 2,000.
  2. Prizes: Winning team of SQUI GCC CUP will get a Samsung Galaxy Note9.
  3. The winners will advance to SQUI CHALLENGE Global Finals.

Requirements:

  1. All team members should have the nationality of one of the counries : Nepal ,Bhutan, India, Sri-lanka. Countries included: Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and the United Arab Emirates.
  2. Registrations are on the first-come-first-serve basis and will close after 800 teams have registered.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION:

The information will strictly be used for contacting players for SQUI CUP only. The tournament team guarantees that the information will be securely stored.
Only team leaders can register for the tournament. Repeated registrations are not allowed. The application cannot be modified once submitted.
A team will be disqualified if any team member is found to have a nationality outside of India, Bhutan, Sri-lanka, Nepal, Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and the United Arab Emirates.
